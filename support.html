Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → select GymFusion → Cancel Subscription. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

How does the AI meal scanner work?

Point your camera at any meal and our AI will analyze the food to estimate calories, protein, carbs, and fats. Results are estimates and may vary based on portion sizes.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your health and fitness data is stored securely using Firebase with encryption. We never sell your personal data to third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open GymFusion → go to the More tab → Settings → Restore Purchases. This will restore any active subscriptions linked to your Apple ID.

Does GymFusion work with Apple Health?

Yes. GymFusion syncs with Apple Health to track your steps, sleep, and weight data. You can enable or disable this in your iPhone's Health app permissions.

How accurate is the AI body fat analysis?

The AI body composition analysis provides estimates based on visual analysis of your progress photos. It is intended for tracking trends over time and should not be considered a medical measurement.
